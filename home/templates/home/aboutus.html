About Us

The UW Stout Spikeball Club is a group focused on having fun by playing Spikeball! We usually try to run a weekly pickup game and a weekly league each semester. We also want to run a tournament each semester that is open to off campus competitors. Our club has members of all skill levels, so if you're new to Spikeball, or a veteran player, we'd love to have you. For more information on meeting times, consider joining our Connect Page.
